How To Use

Features

  • Game: Allows you to enter scores throughout your game.
  • Highscores: Lists the top 10 scores from your games.

Keeping Score

  1. Click New Game
  2. For each player, enter their name and hit Add Player.
  3. When all players are entered, click Start.
  4. Enter each player's score as you work your way through the course. Hit Submit when finished.

Highscores

  • The highscores feature keeps track of the current high scores. After each game, this list is automatically updated.

  • NOTE: the list currently does not support saving after a refresh, this will be added soon!
